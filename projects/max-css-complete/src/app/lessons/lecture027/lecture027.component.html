Margin Collapsing

  • Margin Collapsing occurs when two elements are adjacent to each other
  • The margins of the two elements are Collapsed into one margin
  • The width of the collapsed margin is equal to the largest margin of the two elements
  • For this reason, it's a good idea to use specific properties like margin-top, margin-botton
